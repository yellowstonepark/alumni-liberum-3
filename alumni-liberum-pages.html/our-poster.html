The poster that you are seeing below too a lot of thought and effort to think of. And yes, there is one tiny, tiny spelling error in the Copyright - but that doesn't matter much. Right now, I want to talk more about the symbolizism and what's on the poster.

At the very bottom, you can see our core beliefs outlined. My administration and I will do the best job that we can to fulfill those beliefs. We truly want to improve your education during this difficult time and I promise you, we will do whatever it takes for us to get there. I hope that you seeing this website can help assure you that we will keep our promise. If you Pick Otzar for President, Remember that it's Evident that He Will Be Excellent!
