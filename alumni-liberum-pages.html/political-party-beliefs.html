Our Top 10 Strongest Beliefs (and how we will achieve them in the classroom):

  1. Education
  2. Student's (and teacher's) Rights
  3. Respect/Kindness
  4. Equality
  5. Dedication
  6. Peace in our class and peace with others
  7. Fun Activites
  8. Less Screen Time
  9. Good Foreign Relations
  10. Extra Credit

How will we achieve each of these beliefs:

Education For All

    I will create a study sheet for everyone to study from. This study sheet will be able to found on this website.

Student's (and teacher's) Rights

    I will make sure that everyone know their rights.

Respect/Kindness

    I will enforce the laws that Congress makes about respect and kindness.

Equality

    I will make sure that everyone is treated fairly, and treated the same no matter what gender, race, or ethnicity they are. If I see any injustices, I will speak out.

Dedication

    I will work as long as needed to give you all the best education and school experience that you could have.

Peace in our class and peace with others

    I will make sure that we have peace in our class, and that everyone is being respectful to one another. I also intend to set up peace treaties.

Fun Activites

    I will make sure that we have fun activites that we can participate in. These will include fun games during class (but of course also educational, and relating to the topic).

Less Screen Time

    I will do my best to reduce screentime by taking attendence for the teachers, creating a study sheet (read more about it under the "goals" section), and talking to teachers.

Good Foreign Relations

    I will make sure that we will have good foreign relations with other countries and make sure that we have a relationship that beneifts both of us.

Extra Credit

    I will give extra credit according by the Constitution's rules. With that, I will also give 3-5 pieces of homework per month, because right now, we don't have a long of homework to give extra credit on.
